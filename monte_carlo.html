Each simulation draws independent annual returns from a normal distribution with the specified mean and volatility. Returns apply before withdrawals. Withdrawals inflate each year at the stated inflation rate. Contributions apply during accumulation (before retire age) only. Social Security, taxes, and asset-class correlation are not modelled. Results are illustrative — not a forecast or financial advice.
